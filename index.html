I am a second year Bioinformatics PhD Student in the McCall Lab at Boston University.
I develop computational methods to study the mechanisms that orchestrate developmental cell death and clearance in the brain and ovary of Drosophila melanogaster. I work with a combination of high-throughput techniques, specifically bulk and single-cell RNA-sequencing, in addition to mass spectrometry-based proteomics.
